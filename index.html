Bachelor of Hotel Management — The 4-Year Journey

Duration 4 Years (8 Semesters)
Eligibility 10+2 any stream, 50%+
Affiliation MAKAUT, UGC, NCHMCT
Intake 60 seats per campus
1

Year 1 — Foundation

Food Production I • F&B Service I • Front Office I • Housekeeping I • Hotel Accountancy • Communication • Computer Fundamentals

2

Year 2 — Skill-Build + Industrial Training

Live 24-week internship at a 4/5-star hotel — Taj, ITC, Hyatt, Oberoi, JW Marriott class. Stipend paid. Real guests, real service, real skills.

3

Year 3 — Specialisation

Advanced Food Production • F&B Controls • Hotel Accounting • Tourism Marketing • HR Management • Electives: Bakery / Patisserie / Bar Operations

4

Year 4 — Leadership & Placement

Strategic Hospitality Management • Entrepreneurship • Research Project • On-campus + Off-campus placements with hotel chains, cruise lines, QSR brands, airlines.
